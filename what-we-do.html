WHAT WE DO

Mining services

Oretshotse Mining Services provides a range of specialist mining services.

Contract mining

Oretshotse Contract Mining provides underground mining companies with outsourced mining services such as sweeping and vamping.

Open cast mining

Oretshotse Open Cast Mining specialises in contract mining to open cast mining operations.

Manafacturing & supply

Oretshotse Manufacturing and Supply manufactures and supplies underground mining consumables like timber railway sleepers.

Engineering

Oretshotse Engineering deals with railroad equipment; repairs and maintenance for underground mining operations.

Investments

Oretshotse Investments represents business development, inside and outside the mining industry. An example is an investment in NHR Mining Timber which supplies timber and steel supports to underground mines.

Our story

We enthusiastically explore the mining industry to find challenges that our energetic, capable people can turn into opportunities by cutting through constraints and consistently performing efficiently.

Our success is measured by the number of people we benefit: our own people, energised by opportunity; those we are privileged to serve; and the communities that are the source of both our inspiration and our endeavour.

Our mission is to beneficiate people: investing in the right people; empowering them; collaborating across all our stakeholders; and giving back meaningfully to those communities in which we work.

We build trust by living our values of honesty, integrity, passion and respect.

The greater good we serve

- The beneficiation of the communities our people call home.
